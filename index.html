About me

My name is Wilhelm Krohmer. I am a digital product designer and dancer. As a designer I want to create web and mobile apps that care about the users people using them. Products should be fun, enable people to solve their problems and make them feel comfortable. As such I strive for ethical, inclusive and privacy-focused products with a clear vision.

In my free time I practice dancing, photography, coding and speaking various languages.
